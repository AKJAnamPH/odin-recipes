PIYANGGANG MANOK

Description:

Piyanggang manok, also spelled pyanggang manok, is a Filipino dish consisting of chicken braised in turmeric, onions, lemongrass, ginger, siling haba chilis, garlic, coconut milk, and ground burnt coconut.

    Ingredients

  • One whole chicken
  • Burnt Coconut or Pamapa
  • Coconut Milk
  • Pepper
  • Sugar
  • Bell Peppers
  • Ginger
  • Garlic and Onions
  • Salt
  • Bubok i.e, special spice from Tausug Tribe
  1. For 20 mins. marinate the chicken with burnt coconut, bubok and garlic.
  2. In a hot pan put oil then saute garlic then onions and ginger all together.
  3. Put the chicken into the saute and wait until it looks crispy.
  4. Once the oil from the chicken emerged, add the coconut milk and let it simmer.
  5. Taste and add more of the bubok as necessary flavor to your liking.
